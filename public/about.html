About SellKaro

A simple, safer way to sell your car or anything else.

SellKaro started with a simple problem: selling an old car or any second-hand item online usually means handing out your phone number to dozens of strangers, fielding spam calls, and hoping a listing site doesn't get flooded with fake or low-quality ads.

We do things a little differently. Every ad posted on SellKaro is reviewed by our team before it goes live. When a buyer wants to know more about something, their message comes to us first — we review it and connect the right people, so sellers aren't stuck fielding spam.

Posting an ad is always free, with no limit on how many you can list. We only ask for a small 5% commission on the final price, and only once your item actually sells.

What you can sell

While we started with cars, you can list almost anything — bikes, mobiles, electronics, furniture, property, and more. The same simple process and 5% commission applies across every category.
